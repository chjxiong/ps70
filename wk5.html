3d Scanning
I first tried to scan a mouse, but the scanner was not able to detect anything even though I put a white paper underneath. So I turned to scan a brighter thing. The result was not as good as I imagined. I needed to manually change and modify many things to adjust the result. The color could not be scanned perfectly, and it generated a lot of unnecessary things around the object. After exporting, the mesh should be modified in rhino as well to make it look great, even though the color was still not perfect.
